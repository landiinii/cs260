Who is Landiinii?

Landinii is a Semi-Amature Selfie Photographer local to Provo Utah. He got involved in photography almost 4 years ago after he came back from the gym one day, and decided to take a 'progress pic' of himself. Ever since he has continually improved. You could even say he has mastered his skills.

What does he do?

Landiinii Specializes in a very particular segment of Photography know as mirror selfies. Mirror selfies really started to gain popularity with the 21st century development of Selfies, but the first was actually taken as early as 1889! Landiinii take mirror selfies to a further level, however, with unique outfits, hairstyles, and facial expressions
